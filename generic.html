About Us

Our history

Broker Man Australia was established in 2001 to provide a personal & professional Insurance Broking service, providing access to a wide range of General Insurance products from across the Insurance market both within Australia and overseas.

Our ways of working

Our fundamental objective has always been for Broker Man Australia to provide our clients a consistently high level of service to add value to their business. We achieved this by:

  • Becoming familiar with the client’s business so we can work together to assess their insurance requirements.
  • Carrying out reviews of policies available so we can select those that suit the client’s needs and create a tailored package.
